# Deliverable — Brand Quick Reference

## Name
Deliverable

## Tagline
AI video solutions for businesses that move fast.

## Color
- Primary amber: `#E8940A`
- Amber tint background (badges, active tab): `#FEF3E2`
- Amber tint border: `#F5C97A`
- Amber dark text (on tint backgrounds): `#854F0B` / `#633806`
- Body text: `#111111`
- Secondary text: `#555555`
- Muted text: `#777777` / `#999999`
- Borders: `#e5e5e5` / `#e8e8e8` / `#ebebeb`
- Alt section background: `#fafafa`
- Pain/contrast red (used only in comparison table): bg `#fef2f2`, border `#f5c6c6`, text `#A32D2D`

## Typography
- Font: Inter (Google Fonts), fallback Helvetica Neue / Arial / sans-serif
- Logo/wordmark: weight 500, letter-spacing slightly tight (-0.5px)
- Headings: weight 500, letter-spacing -0.4px to -1.5px depending on size
- Body: weight 400, regular line-height 1.6-1.8

## Voice and copy rules
- No em-dashes. Use commas or split into separate sentences.
- Direct, confident, no fluff. Plain language over jargon.
- Speak to outcomes (time saved, consistency, cost) not tools or "AI" buzzwords as the headline pitch.
- Address the obvious objection directly rather than avoiding it (e.g. "you could do this yourself" gets named, not dodged).
- Avoid "AI solution" as a standalone phrase where possible, lead with the result and mention AI as the method.

## What this brand is NOT
- Not a mass social content / faceless YouTube channel farm
- Not a tool reseller or course-seller
- Not trying to look like a big agency, small and credible is the goal
- Not dark mode, ever. Always renders light.

## Logo treatment
Wordmark only, no icon, no symbol. "Deliverable" set in Inter medium weight, amber color, on white background. Avoid rounded/bubbly display fonts, avoid drop shadows, gradients, or 3D effects.
